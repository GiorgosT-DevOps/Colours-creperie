Colours Crêperie

About Us

A sweet escape in the heart of Kakopetria, created for slow moments, handcrafted flavours, and warm gatherings.

Colours Crêperie blends traditional charm with modern touches, offering a welcoming space inspired by the nostalgic feeling of an old village sweet shop. Located on the lively pedestrian street of Kakopetria, it is the perfect stop for coffee, crêpes, waffles, pancakes, gelato, and freshly made treats.

Whether you are visiting after a walk through the village or looking for a cozy place to enjoy something sweet or savoury, every detail is designed to feel relaxed, friendly, and memorable.
